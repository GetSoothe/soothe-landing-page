Background

SootheTM is a Chrome extension made to help people browse the web without seeing malicious content. Our main goal is to prevent harassement and help users avoid seeing triggers while they browse the web. We believe that no one should have to worry about this online.

The process is simple and subtle: check off the type of content you want to avoid and let us handle the rest! Questionable content is blurred out and if you wish to see it nonetheless you can click on it to reveal.

We developed Soothe in Ottawa, Canada as part of the #HackHarassement challenge sponsored by Major League Hacking. Currently we are working on a beta release.

Features

LIVE UPDATING

Avoid seeing hateful messages in real time (Facebook messages, Twitter etc.)

SENTIMENT ANALYSIS

We will not filter out content until we determine the context, meaning you won't miss out on anything

ADD TERMS

If we happened to miss a word/phrase you can tag it and help us become a better tool!

PERSONALIZED

Only check off the content that makes you uncomfortable and we will help you avoid it.
